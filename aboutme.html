Hobbies & Interests

Sports

I have been playing cricket since my primary school days and have always been fond of the game. I would always find myself going to cricket clubs and playing cricket in the streets.
Now I play with a local league cricket team, they host training/matches every Sunday.
Mainly I play the role of batsmen but consider myself an all-rounder and can sub out a bowler if needed.
On Saturdays I help run a youth club with 4 other members of staff. We run different activites, sports and camps for about 20 youths.

Outdoors

Occasionally I like to go hiking, bouldering or camping. Its a really good stress reliever and just gets you feeling better.
I've hiked/camped many areas in the Peak District and Snowdonia. I try to go atleast once a month with friends/family and have plans to hike Ben Nevis.

But also enjoy open world and survial games games. (Valheim, Terraria, Skryim, Fallout). Check out my recommendation page for my opninion on some games and their respective download links.
